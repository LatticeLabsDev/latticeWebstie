Lattice Labs Academy

Explore Solutions

Lattice Labs empowers the world’s leading brands, institutions, conglomerates, and governments to develop and scale global solutions using interoperable blockchain technology.

A wide range of industries can benefit from Lattice Labs unique and future-ready developed infrastructures.

Take a dive into real-world use cases

Housing & Land Registry

By digitaly tokenising physical real world property assets, the Lattice Network offers the capability of owning, buying, and selling real physical property titles via ledger technology. This engages errorless and seamless registrations of property assets.



Healthcare

Bring transparency, simplicity and efficiency to all healthcare. Lattice Lab's use cases in the healthcare industry provides solutions for a wide range of areas. Including permission sharing of patient data, multi-party authentication of prescription drugs, verification of medical studies with zero knowledge proof and a seamless medical supply chain, alsways ensuring compliance, safety and security.

Central Banking Solutions

For Central Banks to go fully digital with the issuance of Central Bank Digital Currencies (CBDC’s), a blockchain infrastructure is required for longevity of the public's trust. Lattice Network’s infrastructure allows Central Banks and financial institutions to implement an interoperable blockchain framework for the scalability of CBDC’s as a main form of payment without the use of intermediaries.

Institutional Finance

Bring transparency, simplicity and efficiency to all your financial transactions. What if we could revolutionize the comercial financial world by replacing old processes and paperwork with new methods of cooperation, innovation, and speed? What if fraud and crime could be reduced by a shared ledger of truth that is highly secure? Blockchain for institutional finance answers those questions.

Digital Asset Distribution

Lattice Labs solutions for creating and distributing digital assets can change the industry. We welcome you to takea dive into the process, and guide creatives through blockchain. Blockchain technology enables tokenasation of creative digital assets that can now be distributed interoperably through Web3 at a higher speed with an overall lower cost, allowing creatives to track monetastion with full custody of rights on a public ledger. .

Citizen Identity

Lattice Labs offers blockchain solutions that enhance government efficiency and transparency. Our technology eliminates duplicate data, enables seamless communication, and provides user-friendly government platforms. With increased data privacy through self-sovereign identity authentication, citizens have control over the data they share, ensuring it is not accessed without their consent.

Enterprise HR

Lattice Labs offers efficient solutions for transparent company operations, using blockchain technology to eliminate duplicate data and facilitate seamless communication. Their employee-friendly HR platforms prioritize data privacy through zero-knowledge proof, enabling enterprises to streamline internal processes.



Supply Chain Management

It’s pivotal for supply chains to be as resilient, resolute and transparent in this day and age. That’s why Lattice Labs provides tools to facilitate supply chain management that minimize disruptions and delays via smart contracts. This helps to track things such as produce, fruit and vegetables from farms, right the way to production lines and eventually reaching supermarkets.

Decentralised Artificial Intelligence (DAI)

Within the vast landscapes of Artificial Intelligence and Artificial General Intelligence, Lattice Labs create solutions that can bridge blockchain processes with AI and AGI tools for enteprise and accountability operations. These tools can be used across a wide range of industries from healthcare to financial services.

Physical Asset

By transforming physical, real world asset ownership into digital smart contract titles, Lattice Labs has developed ledger software that offers the capability of owning a digital representation in smart contract form of the physical asset ready for easy distribution or easy transfer of owenship. This covers physical assets such as cars, artwork, photos, and so much more. The possibilities are endless.
