Lattice Supply Chain Solution

It’s pivotal for supply chains to be as resilient, resolute and transparent in this day and age. That’s why Lattice Labs has developed a whitelabel solution to facilitate supply chain management that minimizes disruptions and delays via blockchain technology. This helps to track things such as produce, fruit and vegetables from farms, right the way to production lines and eventually reaching supermarkets.

Lattice Supply Chain Demo Contact Us

Take a dive into real-world use cases

Logistics companies are in a highly competitive environment, and they’re under constant pressure to keep costs low. As a result, many logistics companies take advantage of outsourcing - hiring outside businesses or individuals to handle some portion of their operations - to cut down on overhead expenses.

Because of these issues, data sharing is limited, transparency is low due to unknown go-betweens who take on tasks as subcontractors. Additionally supply chain management inefficiencies arise from unstandardised processes and lack of available data.

Logistical issues are exacerbated by the dominance of manual labor and paper-based processes, which comprise a significant portion of current logistics operations.

The Identifiable problem

The complexity of logistic operations makes it difficult to identify and remove counterfeited products from the market.

Counterfeit products

Collateral damages

Fake goods can not only damage the brands they are made to imitate, but low-quality products also get associated with brand names - this problem is especially severe in the case of pharmaceuticals.

Damage to brands

Damage to reputation

A health risk

According to Interpol, around 1 million people die from counterfeit drugs every year. The problem is especially severe in emerging markets, where up to 30 percent of pharmaceuticals are fakes based on the international police agency's research.

The LATTICE Network solution

Blockchain technology can address the serialization issue by creating a tamper proof ledger, tokenizing every attribute to the life cycle of the product or service, and safely saving smart contracts in blocks. This single source of truth improves data availability and transparency, allowing for easy verification.

Digitally efficient

By digitizing processes, companies can make them cheaper, faster and more reliable.

Significant time and cost savings

Fraud-proof

For companies

Companies gain a number of advantages by utilizing blockchain technology, including greater efficiency and reduced risk of counterfeiting; access to more comprehensive and real-time data; and the ability to build direct relationships with consumers through tracing apps.
