Lattice Labs Academy

Developed by Lattice Labs

Lattice Labs builds highly scalable and sustainable blockchain solutions for idustry sectors around the world without ever sacrificing on security. Lattice Labs Dapps include platform whitelables built for some of the world’s most known brands and organizations, now using the most advanced interoperable blockchain technology.

Explore Lattice Labs developed applications.

Lattice Network

The First Interoperable Layer-0 Blockchain Network As A Service (NaaS). A highly scalable non-profit eco-friendly Layer-0 blockchain framework for industry sectors to scale.

Lattice Explorer

Lattice Explorer is a dedicated blockchain exploration tool specifically designed for the Lattice Network, that provides comprehensive real-time insights and transparent visibility into to to explore and search blocks on the Lattice Network blockchain for transactions, addresses, and tokens.

Lattice Backpack

Lattice Backpack is an innovative web3 cold wallet that empowers users to securely carry and utilize their digital assets across the web, providing a convenient and reliable solution for managing and transacting with tokens and other decentralized assets.

Coming Soon.

Lattice AGI Chat

Lattice AGI Chat is an advanced artificial intelligence chatbot utilizing the power of lattice-based algorithms to engage in intelligent and natural conversations with users, offering an unprecedented level of understanding and responsiveness. It also harnesses its extraordinary capabilities to generate captivating images.

Lattice Sports Fan Demo

The Blockchain Sports Fan Platform is an immersive ecosystem that offers sports team fans a gamified experience, engaging them with interactive challenges, rewards, and unique experiences. Additionally, it serves as a robust foundation, providing a wide range of utilities to foster the development of a dynamic sports team metaverse, allowing fans to connect, create, and explore virtual experiences within their favorite team's digital realm.

Lattice Retail Fan Demo

The Blockchain Retail Brand Fan Platform is an interactive ecosystem that offers fans of a specific brand a gamified experience, enticing them with engaging challenges, exclusive rewards, and immersive activities. Moreover, it serves as a comprehensive foundation, equipped with a myriad of utilities, to foster the development of a vibrant metaverse for the brand, enabling fans to connect, collaborate, and explore unique virtual experiences within the brand's digital realm.

Lattice Enterprise HR Demo

The Enterprise HR Platform leverages blockchain technology to offer a secure and efficient ecosystem for enterprises to issue, manage, and verify zero proof credentials, enhancing privacy, streamlining identity verification processes, and enabling seamless access to resources and services within the organization.

Lattice Government Identity Demo

The Government Identity Platform revolutionizes identity verification and authentication for a country or municipalities citizens, by leveraging blockchain technology to provide a secure and decentralized ecosystem for issuing and managing government-issued zero proof credentials, ensuring privacy, trust, and seamless verification processes for citizens across various government services.

Lattice Move To Earn Fan Demo

The Move to Earn platform provides individuals with an opportunity to earn rewards and incentives by actively participating in decentralized protocols and contributing to the growth and development of the blockchain ecosystem, fostering financial inclusivity and empowering users to enhance their economic prospects.

Lattice Land Registry Demo

The Blockchain Land Registry Platform revolutionizes traditional land registry systems by harnessing the immutable and transparent nature of blockchain technology to provide a secure and decentralized platform for recording, verifying, and managing land ownership rights, transactions, and transfers.

Lattice Real Estate Demo

The Real Estate Tokenization Platform leverages blockchain technology to facilitate the fractional ownership and digitization of real estate assets, enabling investors to securely and transparently trade and manage property-backed tokens, unlocking liquidity and expanding investment opportunities in the real estate market.

Lattice Healthcare Demo

The Blockchain Smart Healthcare Platform integrates cutting-edge blockchain technology to create a secure and interoperable ecosystem for the healthcare industry, enabling seamless sharing and management of patient data, enhancing privacy, streamlining medical record access, facilitating precision medicine, and fostering collaborative research and innovation among healthcare providers.

Lattice Supply Chain Demo

The Blockchain Supply Chain Platform revolutionizes the traditional supply chain industry by leveraging the transparency, traceability, and immutability of blockchain technology, enabling end-to-end visibility, real-time tracking, secure transactions, and seamless collaboration among stakeholders, fostering trust and efficiency throughout the supply chain ecosystem.

Lattice Event Demo

The Blockchain Event Fan Platform offers event enthusiasts an exhilarating gamified experience, encompassing interactive challenges, enticing rewards, and immersive activities. Furthermore, it serves as a robust foundation, equipped with a diverse array of utilities, to facilitate the development of a captivating metaverse for the event. This metaverse enables fans to connect, engage, and explore dynamic virtual experiences within the event's digital realm, fostering a sense of community and excitement.

Lattice AI Studio

The Lattice Labs AI Studio is a comprehensive platform that offers developers a wide range of APIs to seamlessly integrate various AI tools developed by Lattice Labs into their platforms and websites, empowering them to enhance functionalities, automate processes, and deliver exceptional user experiences with ease.

Coming Soon.

Lattice Token Studio

The Launchpad is an all-in-one platform that empowers users to design, create, and mint tokens and digital art assets on any blockchain network, providing a user-friendly interface, robust customization options, and seamless integration with multiple blockchain protocols, enabling individuals and businesses to unleash their creativity and tap into the vast potential of decentralized economies.
